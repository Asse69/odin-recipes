Spanacho

Description

Lovely pasta with creamed spinach, melted feta and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (preferably spaghetti)
  • Canned or fresh tomatoes, cherry or otherwise
  • Feta without too much water, so it can melt
  • Creamed spinach

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in pot
  2. Heat up spinach
  3. Chop feta into small chunks and add to already hot spinach
  4. When feta is almost completely melted, add tomatoes
